Githeri

Description

Githeri (GÄ©theri), is a Kenyan traditional meal of maize and legumes, mostly beans of any type mixed and boiled together.

Githeri is a simple Kenyan meal enjoyed in most Kenyan homes. Full of nutrients, the delicacy is considered one of the balanced meals to have on a tight budget.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled beans
  • 1 cup boiled maize
  • 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 2 tomatoes(diced)
  • 5 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped carrot
  • 5 large potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 table spoon tomato paste
  • 1 small bunch chopped fresh coriander
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Heat cooking oil in a medium sized pan/sufuria. Add the garlic and fry until fragrant.Fry for about one minute ,taking care not to burn.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until it is slightly browned and fragrant then add in the diced tomatoes,tomato paste and salt.Stir to combine.
  3. Next,add potatoes,chopped carrot and water then stir.cover and allow to cook for about 10 minutes,or until the potatoes are slightly tender,but not entirely cooked though.Make sure to stir from time to time.
  4. Finally,add the boiled maize,boiled beans.Garnish with some chopped coriander,serve and enjoy while still hot.
